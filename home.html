PyLadies is coming to Australia!

PyLadies is an international mentorship group with a focus on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community.

Share the news!

Why PyLadies?

Attracting more women to code in Python will strengthen the Australian Python community and inspire women to lead and participate in the open source community.

Our goal is to make this group welcoming and encouraging for women to learn, explore and build amazing things in Python.

How will it work?

How PyLadies Australia will run is entirely dependent on our participants. Some of our initial ideas include:

  • Remote learning
    Either guided by experienced Pythonistas, or self-taught using widely available resources.
  • Local meetups
    We're particularly keen on the idea of women meeting prior to attending other Python events. We see this as an easy way for women to gain confidence to attend and contribute.
  • Group Python projects
    Just for fun or to contribute back to the wider Python community or society at large. Games, apps and more!!

Sounds Great!

How can I get involved?

We're looking for PyLadies, PyGents and interested organisations to help kickstart our Australian branch. Perhaps you could help by:

  • Joining the group or starting a local PyLadies group
    Get involved! The more participants we have, the higher the chance of a thriving community. All learner levels are welcome - including those who've never coded before!
  • Shaping the direction of our events and activities
    To best service the Python community we'll need input on how to best advocate for women in Python. What ideas can you bring to the table?
  • Teaching Python classes

  • Mentoring a PyLady
    From code review to social connections, mentoring is a great way to open doors for participating in the Python community.
  • Hosting or sponsoring a meetup or event
    All things going well, we'll need physical locations for meetups, the occasional refreshment, and even grants for special events.
  • Organisation and administration
    There's lots to do! From event management to web development, there are a range of ways you can contribute to making PyLadies Australia a success.
